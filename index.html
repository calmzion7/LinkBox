What's inside

Loaded. And fast.

Three months of building, packed into one player. Here's the short version.

01

AI harness, wired into everything

The built-in AI knows your whole library and what's playing right now. Ask it for a vibe and it builds a playlist into your AI area — ready to shuffle. Local or cloud, your call, with memory that sticks.

02

Shuffle that learns you

Weighted-vote ranking drives a smart shuffle, plus a custom shuffle with its own hit-frequency weighting — and a one-tap "skip this playlist" toggle when you want it out of rotation.

03

A library that cleans itself

The −1 Vault auto-pulls tracks you're done with out of sight. Smart Radio pulls fresh tracks and prunes the duds automatically. Dead links don't pile up.

04

Tag system that hides the noise

Auto-sorts Shorts, concert, remix, documentary and cover versions so they stay out of your main library and playlists — unless you want them.

05

Smart orchestrator loop

A loop manager uses local AI to label every track and link in the background, so your whole catalog gets organized without you lifting a finger.

06

YouTube API plugin system

Plug in your own key and pull your own tracks, your way. The player is built to extend, not box you in.

07

Smart iframe player

A custom player surface with real controls and features — not a bare embed. Smooth on desktop, and runs beautifully on iPad too.

08

Color & layout, your way

A full color system and layout manager let you reshape the whole look and feel until it's yours.

09

Built for big libraries

70,000+ links and still snappy. Designed to stay fast as your collection grows, not grind to a halt.
